Main Page

Welcome to sibermerdeka! Here is where the world of the virtual meets the real, and here you can meet others who are interested in the cyber too...

We are a hackerspace based in Pudu, Kuala Lumpur. Members meet every week on Saturdays at our hackerspace, but we are available most days, and we talk about various topics within this cyber era, among them:

  • Computer networks and the World Wide Web
  • Computers and their power and potential to influence society
  • Public and private data safety and privacy and its place in our lives.

On top of this, we have some facilities for use including:

  • Computers and monitors for messing around with!
  • Electronics equipment for working on your own projects!
  • Game consoles and a lounge for kicking back and relaxing in
  • 1 Gbps internet for use :D

Interested? We are based on the 3rd floor on the shoplot at 392B, Jalan Pudu, near the Pudu train station on the Ampang and Sri Petaling Lines.

For more information, contact us at sibermerdeka (at) protonmail.com, or call us on +60 3-9226 0098.
